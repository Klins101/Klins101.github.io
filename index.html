About Me

Currently pursuing my PhD at the University of Hertfordshire. My expertise spans Python, MATLAB, and C++, complemented by proficiency in CAD tools like SolidWorks.

With extensive experience in communication frameworks such as ROS 2, I enable seamless interaction with robotic systems. My research interests encompass adaptive control, data-driven decision-making, and the application of advanced machine learning algorithms to enhance system performance and robustness.

When I'm not immersed in research, you'll find me supporting Manchester United or taking peaceful walks while listening to gospel music.
